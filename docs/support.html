Support Us

Ticket sales cover barely a quarter of the cost of putting on a concert. Your support helps the WPO maintain its adventurous programming of rare and unusual works alongside familiar repertoire, and keeps admission prices accessible to all.

Shop with easyfundraising

You can raise free funds for the WPO simply by shopping online.

Visit easyfundraising.org.uk and a percentage of what you spend with participating retailers will be donated to the orchestra at no extra cost to you.

Friends of the WPO

Becoming a Friend is the most direct way to support the orchestra. Friends receive:

  • A WPO newsletter
  • An interval drink at performances

To find out more or to join, contact our Friends Administrator

Sponsor the Orchestra

The WPO welcomes business sponsors whose support helps us to present ambitious concerts at leading London venues. As a registered charity, we can offer sponsors meaningful exposure to our audience at concerts, in programmes, and online. We are happy to accommodate bespoke proposals.

The WPO is particularly grateful for the continued support of Body Silk Clinic, Hither Green, London.

To discuss a sponsorship arrangement, please contact: wposecretary@gmail.com
