Rehearsals

Rehearsals are held on Sundays, 2:30pm–5:30pm at:

King Solomon Academy, Penfold Street, London NW1 6RX  (map)
Nearest tube: Edgware Road (TFL)

Members must bring a music stand.

Dress code for indoor concerts: Women — long black; Men — black shirt and trousers, no tie, jacket optional.
