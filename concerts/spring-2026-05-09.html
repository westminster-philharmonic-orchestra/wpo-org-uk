Spring Concert 2026

Saturday 9 May 2026

7:30pm

St Giles Cripplegate, Fore Street, London EC2Y 8DA (TFL)

Tickets

Our final concert of the main season is a Spring-themed programme that highlights innovation and intensity.

Lili Boulanger's shimmering miniature D'un matin de printemps offers a radiant start, followed by Bantock's evocative Russian Scenes.

The spotlight then turns to a rare performance of Ruth Gipps's lyrical and beautifully crafted Oboe Concerto with soloist Ewan Millar (kindly supported by Making Music's Philip & Dorothy Green Young Artists scheme).

The season concludes with Elgar's Symphony No. 1, a masterwork of nobility, pathos, and English symphonic brilliance.

Programme

  • Lili Boulanger — D’un Matin de Printemps
  • Bantock — Russian Scenes
  • Gipps — Oboe Concerto. Ewan Millar oboe
  • Elgar — Symphony No. 1
